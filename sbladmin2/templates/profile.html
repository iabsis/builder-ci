About Matrix Notifications

Matrix is an open network for secure, decentralized communication. By configuring your Matrix account, you'll receive build notifications directly in your Matrix client.

How to find your Matrix ID:
  • Open your Matrix client (Element, FluffyChat, etc.)
  • Go to your user settings or profile
  • Look for your Matrix ID in the format @username:server.domain
  • Example: @john:matrix.org or @alice:example.com
Tip: You need to enable notifications to receive build status updates. Your Matrix ID must be valid and accessible for the system to send messages.
